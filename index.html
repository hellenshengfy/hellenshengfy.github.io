Welcome to my personal website! I graduated with a Master’s degree in Financial Engineering and Risk Management from Beijing Foreign Studies University in 2020 after my Bachelor’s degree in Financial English and Finance (double degree) from Centrail University of Finance and Economics . From August 2020 to November 2022, I worked as a financial analyst in the debt capital market and successfully claimed my Level II badge issued by CFA Institute . I am an archaeology enthusiast since childhood and now I am preparing my university applcation for my second Master’s degree (in archaeology). It’s time to give it a go!
