I graduated with a Master’s degree in Financial Engineering and Risk Management from Beijing Foreign Studies University in 2020 after earning my Bachelor’s degree in Financial English (major) and Finance (minor) from Central University of Finance and Economics . From August 2020 to November 2022, I worked as a financial analyst in the debt capital market and successfully claimed my Level II badge issued by CFA Institute . I am an archaeology enthusiast since childhood and I decided to give it a go. Now I am preparing my university application for my second Master’s degree (in archaeology). Do it NOW and leave NO regret in your life!
