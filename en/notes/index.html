1. Archaeological Excavation at Yinxu

I interned in the excavation project at Yinxu, directed by Mr. Niu Shishan , the leading researcher at the Anyang Workstation of the Archaeology Institute, Chinese Academy of Social Sciences. During this internship, I gained extensive knowledge in archaeological excavation, drawing and survey.

Mr. Niu Shishan’s comment:“Fangyuan is a fast learner. She demonstrates skill in bronze vessel painting.”

5. Archaeological Database

  • PDF: Python Code

I participated in the project led by Dr. Sheng Pengfei and Dr. Zhang Menghan (Fudan University). This project aims to build a comprehensive archaeological database and seek for multidisciplinary evidence (pollen/gene/language) of possible migration of the Neolithic communities in the southeast China. I would like to share some python codes I wrote for the project.
